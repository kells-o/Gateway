About New Mexico

New Mexico is a southwestern state located between Texas and Arizona. It has an estimated population of 2,096,829 and is the 36th largest state by population. The northern and eastern area of New Mexico are colder, alpine climate while the western and southern parts are a warmer, arid climate. This is because of New Mexico's location. New Mexico is mostly covered by mountains, high plains and deserts. These beautiful landscapes make for a perfect vacation or even a place to call home.
